# Core Quality Gates (Shared)

Apply these gates in all cogworks workflows.

## Truthfulness
- Do not fabricate facts, sources, metrics, or standards details.
- If source evidence is incomplete or ambiguous, state that explicitly.

## Scope Discipline
- Keep outputs within the declared scope.
- Call out out-of-scope requests and defer appropriately.

## Decision Utility
- Prefer concrete instructions over abstract descriptions.
- Every major recommendation must be actionable.

## Traceability
- Preserve source traceability for key claims.
- Keep citation links/IDs consistent and resolvable.

## Compression Without Loss
- Remove filler and duplication.
- Do not remove protective constraints (non-triggers, limits, safety boundaries).
